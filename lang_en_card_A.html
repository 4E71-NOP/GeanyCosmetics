Geany Cosmetics online


This little tool help you to create Geany themes the easy way. Choose the colors you want and when you are satisfied with the look, press the 'edit' button. It will fill the textarea with the rendered script. Save the content in a file (.conf) and copy this file in your theme directory. It may vary depending on the system.

You may help yourself with this online tool paletton.com.

How to use: By clicking on an element, a color selector will appear. It will let you select a color. Validating the selection will automatically implement the color.
